Predator Cues in Shooterspool

Predator is characterized by its technological innovation in the manufacture of pool cues and shafts. Globally recognized are its REVO Carbon shafts as well as their low deflection shafts such as the 314-2, the Z-2, the 314-3, the Z-3 and the Vantage.

Numerous professional players such as, Darren Appleton, Ralf Souquet, Joshua Filler or Chang Jung Lin among many others, play with a Predator Cue.

We work with Predator Cues to provide our players the experience of play like a professional pool player.
