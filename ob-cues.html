OB Cues in Shooterspool

OB manufactures high performance pool cues and shafts.

Beginning with the OB-1 shaft, OB has been at the forefront of the “Ultra Low Deflection” development for the last decade.

OB has developed also the DigiCue and the DigiCue BLUE, a revolutionary electronic cue training device for improving player performance.

We collaborate with OB Cues to provide you virtual recreations of their cues and shafts so you can get the best perform playing in Shooterspool. Each cue and shaft features its own designs and technical properties, getting the closest game feeling to the real game.
