Looking for a realistic Billiards Game?

Shooterspool - Billiards Simulation

Shooterspool is a billiards game for Windows PC developed for real players. A simulator that allows you to play just as you would on a billiards table.

If you are a billiards player, this is your game!

Physics Simulation

The most realistic billiards game

Our main goal is to provide you a game experience where you are able to play your billiard games as you do in a real billiards table, where the strategy and the cue ball control are essential.

Either you play billiards in real life or not, Shooterspool will help you to improve game skills such as the aiming, the cue ball control, the strategy and your game routines.
